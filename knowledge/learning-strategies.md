# Effective Learning Strategies

To learn effectively, you need to move beyond passive reading. Here are some actionable tips:

## 1. Active Recall
Instead of re-reading your notes, test yourself. Ask questions and try to answer them without looking at the source.

## 2. Spaced Repetition
Review information at increasing intervals. Use tools like Anki or simple calendar reminders.

## 3. The Feynman Technique
Try to explain a complex concept to a child. If you can't, you don't understand it well enough yet.

> "If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough." — Albert Einstein

### Action Items
- [ ] Pick one topic you're learning today.
- [ ] Explain it to a friend or write a simple summary.
- [ ] Schedule a review for 3 days from now.
